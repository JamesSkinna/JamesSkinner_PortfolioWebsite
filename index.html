    • Hi, I'm James. I am an engineer who loves to solve real-world problems from an interdisciplinary perspective, and design products that address the key user needs.

    *Fitbit Balance
    A healthcare wearable that directly counteracts hand tremor and encourages positive habits to further benefit the user's wellbeing.
    Steady Grip
    A modular, handheld device, designed to allow users with hand tremor to more easily interact with everyday items.
    Bike Frame FEA
    Computer-aided design of a bike frame, using Finite Element Analysis to improve the fatigue life and increase the natural frequency.
    Protec
    A noise monitoring device and system that integrates into existing PPE, aiming to eliminate uncertainty within the construction industry.
    Robo-Bug
    A 6-legged hexapod, with an insect-inspired walking gait, created using cardboard as the primary material.
    Leon & On
    A reusable packaging system, featuring foldable packs to be returned to store for future use - created for the 'DESSOC X Leon' Hackathon
    Mini Segway
    A self-balancing segway that also 'dances' in time with music, demonstrating my understanding of electronics and PID control.
    Space Hack
    A deployment system to lower Spacebit's Asagumo rover onto the lunar surface - created for the 'Making for the Moon' hackathon.
    Mother Nature
    Redesigning the digital pregnancy test as a reusable device which works within a more sustainable product-service system.
